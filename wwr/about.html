Whitewater Rafting

Providing an awesome and exciting white water rafting experience along several of Utah's beautiful rivers. Enjoy the breath-taking scenery and views of various wildlife. With five levels available ranging from calm to insane, there is an adventure ready for anyone. Plan your tour with us for an experience you will never forget.

History

Our company has been guiding white rafting tours since before 2010. We started by gathering family and friends to share in our whitewater adventures. Soon we were being asked if we could host weekly whitewater rafting trips by people who heard the stories of those early adventures. We now offer this amazing experience to you! Join us for a whitewater rafting trip you will never forget! We are so sure you will love your white rafting tour! Book today!
